## Important: Fix the Authentication Error

I see you're getting this error: "412 Gmail_API: Request had insufficient authentication scopes"

**To fix this:**
1. Click "Disconnect" next to your Gmail account
2. Click "Connect Account" again
3. When Google asks for permissions, make sure to allow **"Send email on your behalf"** permission
4. Complete the connection
5. Then click "Create Service"

This should resolve the authentication error.
