# Contact Form Options

Since your portfolio is hosted on GitHub Pages (static site), you need a service to handle email sending. Here are your options:

## Option 1: EmailJS (Current Setup) ✅
**Pros:**
- Free tier: 100 emails/month
- Easy setup (5 minutes)
- Works with Gmail, Outlook, etc.
- No backend needed

**Cons:**
- Requires EmailJS account setup
- Limited to 100 emails/month on free tier

**Status:** Already integrated in your code, just needs keys

---

## Option 2: Formspree (Easiest) ⭐ RECOMMENDED
**Pros:**
- Super simple - no email account setup needed
- Free tier: 50 submissions/month
- Just add form action URL
- No JavaScript needed

**Cons:**
- Free tier limited to 50/month
- Shows Formspree branding on free plan

**Setup:** 2 minutes - just update form action

---

## Option 3: Web3Forms (Simple & Free)
**Pros:**
- Free, unlimited emails
- Simple API
- No account needed (just get access key)
- Privacy-focused

**Cons:**
- Requires access key
- Slightly more setup than Formspree

---

## Option 4: Remove Form, Use Direct Links
**Pros:**
- No setup needed
- No service required
- Always works

**Cons:**
- Users must use their own email client
- Less professional

**What it looks like:** Just show email/phone with mailto: and tel: links

---

## Option 5: Netlify Forms (If you move to Netlify)
**Pros:**
- Free, unlimited
- Built into Netlify hosting
- No third-party service

**Cons:**
- Requires moving from GitHub Pages to Netlify

---

## My Recommendation:

**For easiest setup:** Use **Formspree** - takes 2 minutes, no email account needed.

**For current setup:** Keep **EmailJS** - already integrated, just needs your keys.

**For simplest:** Remove form, use direct email/phone links.
